InVert App

An app designed for amateurs to learn the basics of rock climbing and track

Target Audience

The target audience was identified to be millenials, small families, university students.

Problem Statement

Creating a clean, simple and functional app experience for users to with an appealing aesthetic that helps users navigate through the process was the main goal of app design.

From designing potential user steps, error states, the app was tested with select users to identify potential user experience issues.

Testing with users along the way also helped in identifying pain and friction points. Constant iteration and prototyping helped build the task flows.

Branding

The app icon is a visual personification and identity of an app. The app icon is an iconic representation of a grip, which are essentially holds for the hands and feet while climbing. The line across the climb is indicative of a rope. A simple, yet effective depiction of a indoor rock climbing gym.

The colour palette for the app is synonymous with the colours observed in rocks, stones, cement. It emphasizes the app's message, intended for indoor rock climbers.

App Design

The visual design of an app, when laid out perfectly adhering to the brand icons, colours, visual hierarchy establishes the brand in the user's mind. With appropriate interaction and experience design, the feel of the app is elevated. Functionality is an important part of the app experience. Use of AR in this app elevates the look and feel of the interaction. Clean interface with repetitive gestures for easy navigation is also aesthetically pleasing.
