Born and raised in Chennai, India, I moved to Canada to pursue design at Algonquin College. I have always loved learning new things and am very curious about new products, ideas and technologies. But my gateway to new things apart from the internet has been people and their experiences. Understanding the reason and process behind an action and creating experiences to complement their natural action in the digital & physical world, is something I constantly strive to work and improve on.

My design process is heavily research driven, experimental and strategy focused. I like solving problems by combining design with relevant technologies to create functional products. Striving towards understanding the technical process behind a products helps in creating a meaningful design experience.
